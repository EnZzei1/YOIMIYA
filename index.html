Genshin Impact Theme

Yoimiya is a bot based on the Genshin Impact theme, it generalized several interactive and random functions to let users to experience the same feeling as they play Genshin but differently now,-in a discord server.

Partial functions shown below included wish, fish, openchest, which followed Genshin’s gacha system, but this time Yoimiya will be more GENEROUS and tolerant towards travelers. Also, to have a 10-wish, users need to beg, fight, or open chests to obtain primos, all the data will be saved into MongoDB, compared to free infinite 10-wishes, hopefully, the Yoimiya bot will impact users a high standard of experience.
